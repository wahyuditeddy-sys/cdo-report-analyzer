🧪 Universal Prompt Formula

This formula works across all major AI image platforms:

[SUBJECT] + [ART STYLE] + [COLOR PALETTE] + [OUTLINE TYPE] + [BACKGROUND] + [FORMAT KEYWORDS]

Example: "cute boba tea cup with smiling face, kawaii flat design, pastel pink and purple palette, thick black outline, white background, sticker design, die-cut ready, printable"

🖥️ Platform-Specific Tips

🎨 Midjourney

Append --niji 6 for kawaii/anime style. Use --style raw for cleaner white backgrounds. Add --ar 1:1 for square sticker format. Use --q 2 for higher quality.

🔷 Gemini Imagen

Always include "white background, isolated subject, sticker art" explicitly. Use "vector illustration style" for sharper edges. Avoid complex scenes — one clear subject works best.

🌊 Flow

Flow responds well to style references. Include "flat design" or "cartoon illustration" for consistency. Use negative prompts: "no background, no shadow, no gradient background".

⚡ Stable Diffusion

Use negative prompt: "background, shadow, realistic, photo, blur". Add LoRA like "sticker design LoRA". Works well with SDXL + VAE. CFG scale 7–9 recommended.

📦 Sticker Format Keywords

  • Die-cut ready: "die-cut sticker, clean white border, isolated subject, no background"
  • Sticker sheet: "sticker sheet layout, multiple variations, consistent style, white background"
